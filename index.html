Dave's Coffee & Bread

Dave’s Coffee & Bread is a local food and beverage business focused on serving freshly brewed coffee and artisan bread made with quality ingredients,
founded in the year 2005 which hails from the proud city of Aba.
The product line combines handcrafted breads with carefully prepared coffee to create a welcoming, everyday experience for customers.
Dave’s Coffee & Bread is designed for people who value freshness, comfort, and consistency in their daily meals and drinks.
